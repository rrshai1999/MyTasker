About New Task 

In MyTasker, a task consists of five parts. Please enter task name, the date you wish to complete the task, the priority of the task and the description of the task. Below, you may choose to add sub-tasks if you want to.


About Sub-tasks 

Sub tasks are parts of a task that need to happen. You can use this to make a step by step activity list or a to-do checklist. It's all up to you!
